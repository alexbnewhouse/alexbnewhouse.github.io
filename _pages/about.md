Alex is a social scientist who specializes in the study of collective behavior, radicalization, and extremism in online communities. He is a member of the Colorado Laboratory for Users, Media, and Networks and an affiliate of the Institute of Behavioral Science. He holds the following degrees:

* MS in Analytics, Georgia Institute of Technology
* MA in Nonproliferation and Terrorism Studies, Middlebury Institute of International Studies in Monterey
* BA in English and Political Science, Middlebury College
